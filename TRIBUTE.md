# In Recognition of William Cross

This project exists because of William Cross.

The NDASA Foundation's online giving programme began with his work. The original donation application &mdash; the decision to build it, the commitment to ship it, and the donors it served &mdash; is the foundation on which everything here stands. The present codebase is a secure rebuild; the purpose it serves is his.

Specific lines of code evolve. Intent does not. The choice to make it possible for supporters to give to the NDASA Foundation online originated with William, and his contribution remains part of this system in every meaningful sense.

This platform is finalized and maintained in his honor.
