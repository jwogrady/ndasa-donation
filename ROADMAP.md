# Roadmap

Forward-looking work for the NDASA Donation Platform. Items listed here are candidates, not commitments &mdash; each one is a new piece of scope that would need its own planning, review, and release cycle.

## Next (User Experience)
- Recurring donations (monthly giving)
- Apple Pay / Google Pay / Link
- Share / social distribution
- Campaign-specific donation pages

## Next (Admin / Operations)
- Payment link management
- Donation analytics (trends, breakdowns)
- Campaign tracking
- Export tools

## Later
- Customer portal (Stripe)
- Multi-currency support
- Fraud insights (Radar)
- Advanced reporting
